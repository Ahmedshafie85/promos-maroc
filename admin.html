🚀 Quick start

1. Add your stores first (Marjane, Carrefour, etc.) in the Stores tab.
2. Add categories you want to use (food, electronics, etc.) in the Categories tab.
3. Now you can add deals in the Deals tab — pick the store, category, upload a photo.
4. Anything you add appears LIVE on the public site immediately.
